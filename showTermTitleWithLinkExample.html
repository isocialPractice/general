Show Term Title with Link

Hover over the bold words below to see how setting title attribute will alter how the funciton created title block.


onmouseover="showTermTitleWithLink(this, this.dataset, this.dataset.title)" title="Change the definition."
ONE

onmouseover="showTermTitleWithLink(this, this.dataset, this.dataset.title)" title="Change the definition.:: https://example.com"
TWO

onmouseover="showTermTitleWithLink(this, this.dataset, this.dataset.title)" title="Change the definition.:: Some Article, John Smith::https://example.com"
THREE

onmouseover="showTermTitleWithLink(this, this.dataset, this.dataset.title)" title="Change the definition.:: Some Article, John Smith[l]::https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Smith_(explorer)"
FOUR

onmouseover="showTermTitleWithLink(this, this.dataset, this.dataset.title)" title="Change the definition.:: Some Article[1], John Smith[0]::https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Smith_(explorer)-:-https://example.com"
FIVE

NOTE - As there is really no need for "[l]" after text, index mark in link text can be omitted if links are in correct order.

onmouseover="showTermTitleWithLink(this, this.dataset, this.dataset.title)" title="Change the definition.:: Some Article[0], John Smith[l]::https://example.com-:-https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Smith_(explorer)"
FIVE
